INSTRUCTIONS

  • BOOK: To add a new book to your library press the 'add' button on the top left corner.
    Complete the form with the Title, Author, and Pages submit it and you'll see it right away!
  • DELETE: Double click/tap a book to remove it from your collection
  • FINISHED: You can toggle the state of your books after you add them with the small circle located on the bottom right corner of each one of them
  • RED CIRCLE means you haven't finished it yet.
  • GREEN CIRCLE means you are done with it.
